## Why Create a Cluster First?

Your Kubernetes cluster is the foundation where all your applications, databases, and services will run. Qovery provisions and manages this cluster for you, handling:
- Kubernetes installation and configuration
- Networking, load balancing, and SSL/TLS
- Auto-scaling and high availability
- Security and compliance
- Monitoring and logging
