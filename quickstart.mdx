## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Qovery account ([sign up here](https://console.qovery.com/signup))
- A cloud provider account (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- An application to deploy

## Step 1: Create a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Qovery Console">
    Navigate to [console.qovery.com](https://console.qovery.com) and sign in with your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click on "Create Project" and give it a meaningful name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your cloud provider">
    Link your AWS, GCP, or Azure account to deploy your applications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Deploy Your First Application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add an application">
    Click on "Add Application" and connect your Git repository.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your application">
    Set up environment variables, resources, and deployment settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy">
    Click "Deploy" and watch Qovery build and deploy your application.
  </Step>
</Steps>
