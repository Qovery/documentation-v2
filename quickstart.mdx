## Managed vs Self-Managed

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cluster Managed by Qovery">
    **Best for teams who prefer a hands-off approach**

    **What you get:**
    - Fully managed by Qovery - no Kubernetes expertise required
    - Automatic setup of Kubernetes cluster, autoscaling, DNS, ingress controllers
    - Built-in monitoring and logging
    - Simplified developer experience
    - Qovery handles all maintenance and updates

    **Supported providers:** AWS (EKS), GCP (GKE Autopilot), Azure (AKS), Scaleway (Kapsule)

    **Control:** Limited control over Kubernetes infrastructure (optimized for ease of use)

    **Time:** ~30 minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Self-Managed Cluster (BYOK)">
    **Best for organizations with specific Kubernetes requirements**

    **What you get:**
    - Full control over your Kubernetes setup and configuration
    - Use any cloud provider, on-premise, or hybrid infrastructure
    - Bring your existing cluster or create one with your specifications
    - Same developer experience as managed clusters
    - Greater customization flexibility for organizational needs

    **Supported providers:** Any Kubernetes cluster (AWS, GCP, Azure, DigitalOcean, OVH, on-premise, etc.)

    **Control:** Complete control over infrastructure

    **Time:** ~20 minutes (assuming cluster already exists)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
