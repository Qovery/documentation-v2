## Overview

An organization is a shared account that allows developers to collaborate across multiple projects. Organization owners and administrators can manage all aspects of the organization, from clusters to member access.

## Creating an Organization

### When Signing Up

When you first sign up for Qovery:

1. Sign in through your Git provider (GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket)
2. Create your first organization and project
3. Choose from three available plans:
   - **Free**: For individuals and small teams
   - **Team**: For growing teams with advanced features
   - **Enterprise**: For large organizations with custom requirements

### After Signing Up

To create a new organization after your initial setup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organization Menu">
    Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Organization">
    Click the `+` button in the top right corner of the dropdown menu
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Organization">
    Fill in the required information and select your plan
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Organizations

If you have access to multiple organizations:

1. Locate the organization dropdown in the bottom left of the navigation bar
2. Click on the dropdown to see all available organizations
3. Select the organization you want to switch to

## Organization Settings

### Accessing Settings

Click the "Wheel" button (⚙️) on the left navigation bar to access organization settings.

### General Information

Configure basic organization details:

- **Company Name**: Your organization's official name
- **Description**: Brief description of your organization
- **Website**: Your company website URL
- **Admin Contact Emails**: Email addresses for administrative notifications

## Billing

Manage your organization's billing and subscription:

- **View Invoices**: Access all past and current invoices
- **Payment Methods**: Add or update credit cards and payment details
- **Subscription**: Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- **Usage**: Monitor resource usage and costs

<Info>
For detailed pricing information, visit [Qovery's pricing page](https://www.qovery.com/pricing).
</Info>

## Deleting an Organization

<Warning>
Deleting an organization is a **non-recoverable operation**. All associated data, including projects, environments, applications, and configurations will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

To delete an organization:

1. Navigate to Organization Settings
2. Scroll to the "Danger Zone" section
3. Click "Delete Organization"
4. Confirm the deletion by typing the organization name
