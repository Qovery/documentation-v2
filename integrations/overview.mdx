## Overview

Qovery integrates seamlessly with the most popular tools and services in the cloud-native ecosystem. Connect your existing infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, container registries, observability tools, and more to create a complete DevOps workflow.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Kubernetes">
    Choose between Qovery-managed clusters (AWS, GCP, Azure, Scaleway) or bring your own Kubernetes cluster (BYOK).

    [Kubernetes Setup →](/integrations/kubernetes/overview)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link Git Repository">
    Connect GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket to enable automatic deployments from your repositories.

    [Git Providers →](/integrations/git-providers/overview)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure CI/CD">
    Use Qovery Deploy for zero-config builds or integrate with GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or other CI/CD tools.

    [CI/CD Setup →](/integrations/ci-cd/overview)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Observability">
    Start with built-in Qovery Observe or integrate Datadog or other monitoring solutions.

    [Observability →](/integrations/observability/overview)
  </Step>
</Steps>
