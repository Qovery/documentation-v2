Qovery is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our customers and their users. We have implemented comprehensive GDPR compliance measures.

## Data Protection by Design

Qovery maintains internal policies requiring employees to complete security training and follow established protocols. Privacy considerations are integrated into every project from inception.

## Data Protection Officer

Qovery has appointed a Security Officer who also serves as the Data Protection Officer (DPO).

<Card title="Contact Us" icon="envelope" href="https://www.qovery.com/contact">
  Organizations with questions or concerns can reach out through our contact page
</Card>

## Consent Management

All of our customer communication, both business-related and marketing-related, is opt-in, and no information is shared with us without a customer's consent. No data is collected without explicit permission.

## Individual Rights

Qovery ensures compliance with GDPR individual rights including:

- **Data Portability**: Export your data in portable formats
- **Rectification**: Modify account information directly - most account information can be modified directly by users
- **Deletion Requests**: Account deletion is available upon request

Qovery has documented its data collection practices.

Qovery maintains documented retention policies.
